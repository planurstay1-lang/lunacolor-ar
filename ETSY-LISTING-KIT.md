# 🛍️ Etsy Listing Kit — LunaColor AR

## Product concept

**"The coloring book that comes ALIVE"** — each printed page has a QR code.
Kids color it, a grown-up scans it, and the creature rises off the paper as an
animated 3D character in AR. No app install — it runs in the phone browser.

Two product types:

| Product | Format | Suggested price |
|---|---|---|
| Digital download (instant) | PDF, 4+ pages, print at home | $4–7 |
| Physical coloring book | Printed & shipped, 20+ pages | $14–24 |

Digital downloads are the easy start: zero inventory, Etsy handles delivery.
For physical books, print-on-demand services (e.g. Printify/Lulu) or a local
print shop work; use 100–120 gsm uncoated paper (crayon-friendly, matte —
glossy paper hurts AR tracking).

## Description

**Watch your child's face when their drawing stands up and dances! 🤯**

LunaColor AR pages are real coloring pages with a magical secret: after
coloring, scan the QR code with any phone — no app needed — point the camera at
the page, and the creature LEAPS off the paper as an animated 3D character your
child can walk around. Tap "Copy my colors!" and the creature dresses itself
in **your child's actual crayon colors, read straight off the paper**. They can
even ask it questions ("What do you eat?" "How fast are you?") and get fun,
kid-safe answers!

**What's included**
- 🚀 Blast-Off Rocket • 🦋 Flutter Butterfly • 🐠 Bubbles the Fish • 🦕 Dara the Dino
- High-resolution printable PDFs (US Letter & A4)
- Lifetime access to the AR experience — works on iPhone & Android browsers

**How it works**
1. Print & color with crayons, pencils or markers
2. Grown-up scans the QR code on the page
3. Point the camera at the artwork… MAGIC! ✨

**Perfect for:** birthday gifts, rainy days, screen time that starts with
crayons, ages 3–9.

## Operations notes

- Generate the print PDFs from the app: open each page → **Print** → "Save as PDF".
- The AR site must stay online at the URL baked into the QR codes — deploy
  before printing, and keep the domain stable (a custom domain is safest).
- Each new page design needs its image target generated once
  (see README "Adding a new page").
